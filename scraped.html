[Music] Wizkid Ft. Tems & Justin Bieber ‚Äì Essence (Remix)

Posted by OG DEJI. on 13 August, 2021 under Music    109

Music

Starboy Entertainment boss, Wizkid releases the remix to his well-accepted summer anthem “Essence”.

The original song features Nigerian fast rising songstress, Tems. On the remix, the Starboy boss included the services of Canadian singer and songwriter, Justin Bieber.

Wizkid is the second Afrobeat/ Nigerian artiste Justin Bieber would be working with after featuring Burna Boy on the track ‘Loved By You‘.

“Essence Remix” is off Wizkid’s soon to be released studio project “Made In Lagos (Deluxe)”.
